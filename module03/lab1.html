Cristian Chihuahua

The Chihuahua is a Mexican breed of a Toy Dog. The name Chihuahua came from the Mexican state Chihuahua and it's the smallest dog breed in the world. The Chihuahua history says that It has come from Native American Dogs entered North America from Siberia roughly 10,000 years ago, and were then isolated for 9,000 years until the arrival of the Europeans. Chihauhua have a very small head and said to have an "Apple-head" or "Apple-Dome" skull formation. Chihuahuas can come in many different colors like brown, white, tan, and many more. They can also be very fluffy or they can have small hairs. Personally, my Chihuahua is a small dog but has many different features than other Chihuahuas. My Chihuahua is a dark brown color with tan some tan around it's body. He also has white fur on his chest. My Chihuahua can look very similar to another Dog Breed like a Rottweiler. Like many other small dogs, Chihuahuas can act very aggressive toward other people or other dogs.
